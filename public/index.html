What is a story board?

It’s made up of a number of squares with illustrations or pictures representing each shot, with notes about what’s going on in the scene and what’s being said in the script during that shot. Think of it as sort of a comic book version of your script.

View details »

Why do you need it?

A visual aid makes it much easier for you to share and explain your vision for your video with others. When you storyboard a video you’re setting up a plan for production, including all the shots you’ll need, the order that they’ll be laid out, and how the visuals will interact with the script.

Story Board Process

The process begins with using a storyboard template. You then must add the script and then sketches.
